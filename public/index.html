Chicago-style pizza with spinach and mushrooms.

90 MIN

6 / 8 Portions

INGREDIENTS

Dough

  • 20g instant dry yeast.
  • 1 teaspoon sugar.
  • 500ml warm water.
  • 100ml vegetable oil.
  • 4 tablespoons olive oil.
  • 65g fine corn flour (chuchoca).
  • 2 teaspoons salt.
  • 625g flour.

Sauce

  • 5 tablespoons olive oil.
  • 1/2 onion, finely diced.
  • 3 tablespoons Gourmet Oregano.
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons Gourmet Basil.
  • 2 cans of diced tomatoes in natural juice.
  • 2 teaspoons sugar.

Filling

  • 1/2 onion, diced.

PREPARATION

  1. For the dough, dissolve the yeast and sugar in warm water in a large bowl. Let it rest for 2 minutes. Add the vegetable oil, corn flour, salt, and 400g of flour. Mix well. Knead and add the remaining flour, as needed, to have a soft dough. Place it on the counter and knead for 5 minutes or until you have a smooth elastic dough (add flour to the counter as you knead to prevent sticking). Put the dough in an oiled bowl, cover with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise until it doubles in size. Punch down the dough to deflate it and then let it rise for an additional 40 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, prepare the sauce: Heat 4 tablespoons of oil in a pot over medium heat. Add the onion, Gourmet Oregano, Gourmet Basil, salt, and cook until the onion is golden. Add the garlic and cook for 30 seconds. Add the canned tomatoes along with their juice to the onion mixture. Add the sugar and cook until the mixture has reduced and thickened, about 30 minutes. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Set aside.
  3. Filling: Cook the shallots in olive oil until they are translucent. Add the mushrooms and spinach, cook until they are cooked and all the liquid released by the vegetables has evaporated. Set aside.
  4. Preheat the oven to 220Â°C.
  5. Once the dough has rested, divide it into two balls. Roll out each dough to cover two 26 cm (10 inch) removable bottom tart pans, forming a 5cm (2 inch) border with the dough. Divide the filling between the two pizzas, cover with mozzarella cheese, then sprinkle with grated cheese.
  6. Bake for 35 to 40 minutes or until the filling is hot and the base is puffy and golden. Wait a few minutes before unmolding.
